Playing Atari Games with Reinforcement Learning

Made: 02/07/19

My dissertation topic during my final year of studies at the University of Macedonia. Using the python programming language along with Machine Learning libraries like Tensorflow and Keras I implemented two reinforcement learning algorithms in order to train an agent to behave optimally in four game environments.

Source Code

Features:

  • Two Reinforcement Learning Algorithms: Deep Q-Learning and A3C
  • Architecture and Training of a Convoluted Neural Network using Tensorflow and Keras
  • Training on Four Distinct Game Environments using OpenAI Gym
  • Training Results Graphs
  • Comparison of the two algorithms

Monster's Lair

Made: 15/06/2016

A tower defence game I made during the second year of my studies. Unlike traditional games in this game, you take the role of the monsters, and your goal is to block the humans that are invading your lair. To achieve that you have to build towers, upgrade them and strategize on how to stop them in their tracks before they reach the finish line.

Source code.

Features:

  • Build, Upgrade and Sell tower mechanics
  • 3 different types of turrets with their own attacks
  • 3 different enemy types with their own movement and behavior
  • Camera Controls
  • Enemy AI pathfinding movement
  • Different screens: Main Menu, Level Select, Game Over, Victory
  • Animations
